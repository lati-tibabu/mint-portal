Mission

The Ministry of Innovation and Technology (MinT) is committed to leading Ethiopia's transformation into a knowledge-based economy by promoting innovation and technology. We believe that innovation is the key to solving Ethiopia's most pressing challenges, and we are working to create an environment where innovation can thrive.

Vision

Our vision is to make Ethiopia a global leader in innovation and technology. We want to see Ethiopia become a hub for research and development, and we want to see Ethiopian entrepreneurs and innovators creating world-changing products and services.
